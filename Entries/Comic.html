Word: Comic

Definition:

  • English: Pertaining to comedy, as a literary genre.

    Part of Speech: Adjective

    Urdu: ظریف

  • English: A story composed of drawn images arranged in a sequence, usually with textual captions; a graphic novel.

    Part of Speech: Noun

    Urdu: ظریفہ، ناٹکیہ

Etymology:

From Latin comicus, from Ancient Greek κωμικός (kōmikós, “relating to comedy”), from κῶμος (kômos, “carousal”).

Example Sentences:

English: He possesses a comical personality.

Urdu: وہ ظریفانہ شخصیت کا حامل ہے۔

English: The comic was so funny that I couldn't stop laughing.

Urdu: وہ ظریفہ اتنی مزے دار تھی کہ میں ہنسی نہیں روک سکا۔

English: That comic was a huge hit with the audience.

Urdu: وہ ناٹکیہ ناظرین میں بہت مقبول ہوا۔
